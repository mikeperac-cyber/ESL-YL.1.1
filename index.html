📖 Leo's Great Summer Holiday

Instruction: Read Leo's story out loud smoothly. Pay extra attention to the bold words! (Hikayeyi yüksek sesle akıcı bir şekilde oku. Kalın yazılmış kelimelere dikkat et!)
Hi! My name is Leo, and I am eleven years old. Every summer, I travel to a beautiful seaside city on holiday with my family. We stay in a small hotel near the beach. In the morning, I eat a big breakfast with eggs, cheese, and fresh tomatoes.

In the afternoon, I go to the local park because I love sports. I meet new friends there and we play an exciting football match. Yesterday, our team played very well, and I managed to score two spectacular goals! It was amazing!

In the evening, we go to a cozy restaurant. I love to try new things. Yesterday, I ate a special pasta dish. It was very delicious! After dinner, I usually walk by the sea with my parents and look at the bright stars. Daily life here is very fun!
